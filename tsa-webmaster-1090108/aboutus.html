About Us

Our team of three have been dedicated to creating a simple startup restaurant in Seattle, Washington, that serves people with rich, tasty, and high-quality food while also keeping all our food products vegetarian to attain a wider audience. Continue reading further to know a bit more about our team behind the wheel.

Meet the Chefs

We work to maintain a simple budget, trying to spend very little money and be as efficient as possible. That's why we partnered with Veggie Grill. We buy our products fresh from organic farms and send it to the chefs of Veggie Grill, where they prepare wonderful dishes such as those listed on our menu.
